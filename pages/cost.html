Because our mission is to introduce the best students to ancient and modern Rome, the ICCS follows a need-blind admissions policy: we admit students without regard to financial need. In support of our mission we have offered an average of $100,000 per semester to students demonstrating need. Our resources do not yet permit us to completely meet everyone’s demonstrated need, but we do give substantial help, sometimes almost a full scholarship, to everyone with demonstrated need.
