sites

“There is no substitute for seeing things yourself” might as well be our motto. The centerpiece of the ICCS experience is the Ancient City course, in which we visit outstanding Italian and Sicilian sites which loom large in ancient history and literature. Twelve weeks are spent for the most part in Rome and the province of Lazio which surrounds it. One week is spent in Sicily, while another full week is spent in Campania. Depending upon the special interests of the faculty, we sometimes take a trip to Ravenna in northern Italy to see unforgettable early Christian art and architecture.
